As a financial cooperative, FORUM is not publicly traded and doesn’t have shareholders. Instead, our members are owners, and we are able to return profits to members through higher savings rates and lower loan rates. It’s “Your Money. Your Voice.” at FORUM – our members have a say in their financial wellbeing, and we simply provide an opportunity for members to successfully take control of their money and make it work harder for them.
