About FORUM

FORUM Credit Union is more than just an organization. We are family.

Since 1941, FORUM Credit Union has built a reputation based on serving our members and our community. That is more than 75 years dedicated to helping our members live their financial dreams. We have grown from one small branch in downtown Indianapolis to multiple convenient branch locations in central Indiana, serving more than 140,000 members. We have been ranked as one of Indiana’s Best Places to Work since 2012. Much of FORUM’s success is attributable to our people and our culture. Our passion is to demonstrate the heart of what a credit union is – a cooperative where people help people.
