Get a Reduced Rate!

Forum members can get a share secured loan by using the money their savings account as collateral to secure a loan. This type of loan can help a person build credit by repaying the loan amount and allows the funds in your savings account to continue earning interest. The funds in your savings account being used as collateral for the loan are not available for withdrawal until the loan is paid in full. Loan options and rates vary with a share secured loan.
